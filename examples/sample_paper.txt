Example Paper Title

ABSTRACT

This paper studies graph neural networks for node classification. The method uses a message passing architecture and evaluates performance on Cora and Citeseer using accuracy.

INTRODUCTION

Graph neural networks are widely used for learning representations from graph-structured data. Existing methods are often compared with GCN and GraphSAGE on citation network benchmarks.

METHOD

The proposed approach combines neighborhood aggregation with a lightweight attention mechanism.

EXPERIMENTS

Experiments are conducted on Cora and Citeseer. The main metric is accuracy. Baselines include GCN and GraphSAGE.

CONCLUSION

The method improves node classification performance on common citation network datasets.
